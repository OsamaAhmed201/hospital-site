About Us

Welcome to Toronto General Hospital we are here For your care and assistance, we are in a hospital with many departments are equipped with state-of-the-art diagnostic and therapeutic equipment and operated by top-category physicians, Ph.D. and Doctors of Medicine, associate professors and professors knowledgeable of the best available practices of European schools. Partnerships with specialized clinics and research centers in Russia and Europe make possible consultations by doctors of unique specialties.

An operating room is a facility within a hospital where surgeries are performed in a sterile environment. Historically, the term "operating room" referred to a theater or amphitheater that is not sterile or layered, where students and other spectators could watch surgeons perform surgery.

Operations

The Operations Department began to work as one of the most important departments of the hospital since the first day of the opening of this hospital, with 4 operating rooms, and due to the increasing number of qualitative and numerical operations of all kinds and keeping pace with the development of international medical devices and the commitment of the Specialized Hospital to the march of quality and excellence, and due to the importance of the Operations Department in this field, it has The hospital administration is expanding the operations department to 16 rooms that contain the latest technology of design, implementation and equipment.
