Doctors

The job of the doctor is a great job, it is a humanitarian job, he helps patients to improve their health and get rid of their pain. The important role that the doctor plays in society appeared during the Corona pandemic. Doctors were the first barrier to the Corona pandemic, so they did not leave their work for fear of infection and disease, but rather worked seriously, and some of them worked 24 hours to save the sick. Nothing stopped them from their work, even when many of his fellow doctors were injured. We thank the doctors very much, respect them, and appreciate the great effort they made to help patients.
